## Important Guidelines for JSON Generation

1. **Color Adherence**
   - Use ONLY colors from the design theme palette
   - Primary brand color: For headers and main elements
   - Accent color: For CTAs, emphasis, and highlights
   - White: For backgrounds and text on dark backgrounds
   - Black: For body text on white backgrounds

2. **Typography Compliance**
   - Titles: 40-44pt, bold, primary color or white
   - Subtitles: 18pt, regular or bold
   - Body text: 15pt, regular, black or white
   - Never deviate from Arial font family

3. **Layout Patterns**
   - Follow one of the established layout patterns
   - Maintain 35px margins on all sides
   - Use 20px gutters between columns
   - Align content to invisible grid

4. **Emphasis Techniques**
   - Use orange (#F26633) for callout boxes and key metrics
   - Apply orange accent lines (7.64px height) for visual breaks
   - Bold text instead of italic for emphasis
   - Maintain white space around emphasized content

5. **Sales Messaging**
   - Focus on prospect benefits (not just features)
   - Each slide should have one clear message
   - Use specific, relevant data points
   - Build narrative flow across slides
